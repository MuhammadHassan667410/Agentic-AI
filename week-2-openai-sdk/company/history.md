# Company History: Sentinel AI

**2021: The Genesis**
*   Sentinel AI is founded by Dr. Aris Thorne, a renowned AI researcher, and Anya Sharma, a veteran cybersecurity strategist. They identify a critical gap in the market: traditional security solutions are overwhelmed by the volume and sophistication of modern cyber threats.
*   The company is bootstrapped in a small office in Austin, Texas, with a seed team of five engineers. The initial focus is on developing a proof-of-concept for the `ThreatPredict` engine.

**2022: Product-Market Fit**
*   **Q1 2022:** The first MVP of the `ThreatPredict` engine is released to a closed beta group of 10 enterprise clients. The feedback is overwhelmingly positive, validating the core concept of predictive threat intelligence.
*   **Q3 2022:** Sentinel AI secures $15 million in a Series A funding round led by Titan Ventures.
*   **Q4 2022:** The company officially launches its flagship product, the Sentinel AI Defense Platform, featuring both `ThreatPredict` and the new `AutoResponse` module.

**2023: Growth and Expansion**
*   The company grows to 30 employees, expanding its engineering, sales, and security analyst teams.
*   Sentinel AI opens a second office in London to serve the EMEA market.
*   The `ComplianceGuard` module is launched, adding a crucial new capability to the platform and attracting clients in the finance and healthcare sectors.
*   The company is recognized by industry analysts as a "Cool Vendor" in AI-driven security.

**2024: Scaling for the Future**
*   Sentinel AI surpasses 100 enterprise customers.
*   The company secures a $50 million Series B round, earmarking the funds for global expansion and R&D into quantum-resistant AI models.
*   Plans are announced for a new state-of-the-art headquarters in Austin.

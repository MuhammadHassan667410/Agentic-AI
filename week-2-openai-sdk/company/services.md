# Professional Services: Sentinel AI

In addition to our core platform, we offer a suite of professional services to ensure our customers are successful and secure.

## 1. Onboarding & Implementation
*   **Description:** A dedicated 90-day program led by our security engineers to ensure the Sentinel AI platform is seamlessly integrated into your existing security stack. We work with your team to configure playbooks, set up integrations, and train your users.
*   **Goal:** Ensure rapid time-to-value and a smooth transition.

## 2. Incident Response Retainer
*   **Description:** For organizations that need an extra layer of expert support, our IR Retainer provides 24/7 access to our elite team of security analysts. In the event of a major incident that requires human expertise, our team is on standby to assist with deep-dive forensics, threat hunting, and strategic remediation.
*   **Goal:** Provide peace of mind and expert backup for critical security events.

## 3. Security Posture Assessment
*   **Description:** A one-time, comprehensive assessment of your organization's security posture. Our team uses the Sentinel AI platform and other advanced tools to perform a deep analysis of your vulnerabilities, configurations, and processes. We deliver a detailed report with a prioritized list of actionable recommendations.
*   **Goal:** Provide a clear, strategic roadmap for improving your overall security resilience.

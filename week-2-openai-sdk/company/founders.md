# Founders: Sentinel AI

## Dr. Aris Thorne - Chief Technology Officer (CTO)
Aris is the visionary architect behind Sentinel AI's core technology. With a Ph.D. in Machine Learning from MIT and over 15 years of experience in AI research, Aris leads the company's R&D and engineering teams. Before founding Sentinel AI, he was a principal research scientist at a leading technology firm, where his work on neural networks and predictive modeling resulted in 12 patents. Aris believes that autonomy is the key to solving the cybersecurity talent gap and is dedicated to building systems that can reason and act independently.

## Anya Sharma - Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
Anya provides the strategic direction and business leadership for Sentinel AI. A seasoned cybersecurity expert with a background in threat intelligence at both governmental agencies and Fortune 500 companies, Anya understands the real-world challenges CISOs face. She is responsible for the company's growth, strategy, and overall vision. Before co-founding Sentinel AI, Anya was the VP of Security Operations at a major financial institution, where she experienced firsthand the limitations of traditional security tools, inspiring her to build a better solution.

# Company Details: Sentinel AI

**Full Legal Name:** Sentinel AI, Inc.
**Tagline:** Intelligent Defense, Automated.

## Mission Statement
To protect the digital world by providing a revolutionary, AI-driven cybersecurity platform that anticipates threats, automates defense, and empowers security professionals to focus on what matters most.

## Vision
To create a future where cybersecurity is no longer a reactive battle but a proactive, intelligent, and autonomous system that ensures digital trust and resilience for all organizations.

## Core Values
*   **Innovation at the Core:** We are relentlessly curious and committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in AI and cybersecurity.
*   **Customer-Centric Security:** Our success is measured by our customers' security and peace of mind. We partner with them to solve their biggest challenges.
*   **Uncompromising Integrity:** We operate with complete transparency and an unwavering commitment to ethical practices in our technology and business.
*   **Excellence in Execution:** From code to customer support, we strive for the highest quality and reliability in everything we do.
*   **One Team:** We foster a collaborative environment where diverse perspectives are celebrated, and we win or lose together.

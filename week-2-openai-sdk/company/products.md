# Products: Sentinel AI

## 1. ThreatPredict Engine
*   **Description:** A real-time threat intelligence platform that serves as the "brain" of our system. It uses a proprietary machine learning model trained on a massive, diverse dataset of global threat activity. It analyzes network traffic, endpoint behavior, and dark web chatter to predict potential attack vectors *before* they are exploited.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   Predictive Risk Scoring for all assets.
    *   Identification of emerging threat patterns.
    *   Integration with existing security data lakes.
*   **Value:** Moves security from a reactive to a proactive stance, allowing teams to patch vulnerabilities and harden defenses before an attack occurs.

## 2. AutoResponse Platform
*   **Description:** An AI-powered Security Orchestration, Automation, and Response (SOAR) tool. When `ThreatPredict` or another integrated tool detects a credible threat, `AutoResponse` instantly executes a customized, pre-defined playbook.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   No-code playbook builder.
    *   Instant endpoint quarantine and network isolation.
    *   Automated evidence collection and chain of custody logging.
    *   Integration with over 300 third-party tools (Slack, Jira, etc.).
*   **Value:** Reduces mean time to response (MTTR) from hours to seconds. Frees up human security analysts from repetitive tasks to focus on high-level investigation.

## 3. ComplianceGuard
*   **Description:** An automated compliance management tool that provides continuous visibility into your security posture against major regulatory frameworks.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   Pre-built policies for SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA, and ISO 27001.
    *   AI-driven mapping of controls to system evidence.
    *   Automated evidence gathering and report generation.
    *   Continuous monitoring and alerting for non-compliance.
*   **Value:** Drastically simplifies the audit preparation process, reduces audit risk, and ensures the organization remains compliant year-round, not just during audit season.
